# Guides Standard

Rule source for on-demand bundled guides.

## Contract

A guide teaches an authoring or operating agent how to apply canon and standards
to a class of work. It is task-facing and concrete, but it does not become the
only owner of foundational laws, surface standards, or command contracts.

A guide states:

- the surface or workflow it helps with;
- the rule source that owns its judgments;
- when to use the guide;
- the procedure or review sequence;
- what to edit and what not to edit;
- validation or next safe action.

## Boundaries

Guide examples and templates may repeat small derived forms to be usable. The
guide routes durable laws to canon, surface contracts to standards, and exact
command syntax to CLI help.

## Quality Bar

A guide is good when it helps the agent act correctly without hiding the owner
of the law, standard, tool contract, or repair target.
