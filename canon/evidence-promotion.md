# Evidence Promotion

Power: honest memory.

Evidence and doctrine are different kinds of memory.

Decision Question: is this material historical evidence, or has a durable
conclusion been promoted into live doctrine?

## Law

Evidence becomes doctrine only through deliberate promotion:

1. identify the durable conclusion;
2. find the live owner;
3. write the conclusion there in present tense;
4. keep the asset as evidence;
5. do not keep two live copies.

## Failure Signs

- a session recap is cited as live doctrine;
- research notes are rewritten to stay current;
- a conclusion is copied into several live files.
