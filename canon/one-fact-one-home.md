# One Fact, One Home

Power: coherence.

Every durable fact has one live home.

Decision Question: where is the live owner of this durable fact?

## Law

A fact can be a principle, command, path, identifier, workflow rule, limitation,
model, decision, or definition. If the same fact is stated as live truth in more
than one place, drift has begun.

Other surfaces may route to the owner. They do not become parallel owners.

## Failure Signs

- a README repeats a CLI contract instead of pointing to the CLI;
- a routine restates the model it applies;
- a plan or research asset becomes current doctrine;
- two files define the same limitation, path, or command differently.
