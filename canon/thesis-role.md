# Thesis Role

Power: distilled worldview.

A thesis is the public shape produced from foundational principles. It is not
the whole law library.

Decision Question: does this law change the public worldview enough to belong
in the thesis?

## Law

The thesis states the central worldview. It selects, arranges, and distills
canon into a readable model.

A law enters the thesis only when it changes the central claim, the public model,
the language of the paradigm, the boundary with adjacent tools, or the
consequence of adopting ContextKit.

## Failure Signs

- the thesis repeats every canon file;
- the thesis contains procedure or command contracts;
- a local insight is promoted into the public worldview before its role is
  understood.
